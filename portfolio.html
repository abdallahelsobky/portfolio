Enterprise Telecom Data Architecture: OLTP to Data Warehouse

🛑 The Challenge:
High-volume telecom transactional operations databases (OLTP systems keeping records of Calls, SMS, Recharges, and plans) are highly normalized and optimized strictly for short transactional tasks. This baseline setup makes them heavily inefficient and extremely sluggish when executing complex relational queries or populating large-scale historical business intelligence applications.
🛠️ The Solution Architecture:
Designed a fully functional transactional OLTP database and engineered a robust enterprise ETL workflow to extract operations data into a staging environment for validation and deduping logic. Re-architected and modeled the analytical target using an optimized multi-dimensional Star Schema structure. Implemented Slowly Changing Dimensions (SCD Type 2) tracking algorithms to log dynamic changes in customer details over historical arcs while reinforcing performance metrics through custom storage partitioning.
📊 Business Value & Insights:
Established a reliable, production-grade target warehouse layout. Formulated complex analytic SQL queries that surface crucial business dimensions including customer churn curves, monthly revenue trends, service plan performance metrics, and top-tier revenue generating accounts.
Informatica Cloud (IICS) | ETL Design | Data Integration | Data Routing

Multi-Source Cloud Sales Data Integration & Analytics Pipeline

🛑 The Challenge:
Retail sales logs were isolated across separate regional transactional clusters (e.g., Cairo and Alexandria). The ingestion layer suffered from deep data format inconsistencies, invalid datatypes, missing attributes, and duplicate records, creating friction that blocked business groups from auditing centralized sales totals.
🛠️ The Solution Architecture:
Created a resilient, cloud-hosted integration structure leveraging Informatica Cloud (IICS). Merged multi-city transactional streams via Union logic, then embedded stringent Filter and Expression transformation controls to eliminate dirty logs and enforce structural data uniformity. Integrated dynamic Lookup patterns to automate regional tax tier indexing and product dimension matching, grouping total results at the product SKU level through explicit Aggregator layers before deploying a dynamic Router configuration.
📊 Business Value & Insights:
Delivered an automated cloud engineering pipe that continuously populates production target endpoints with clean, trusted data segments (Best Sellers vs Average Products), eliminating operational delay for downstream strategic business units.
